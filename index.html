Official Announcement

Every great search
must come to an end.

After 25 years of answering the world's questions, Ask.com officially closed its doors on May 1, 2026.

The Catalyst

For a quarter of a century, we thrived on your curiosity. But the digital ecosystem is a living, breathing landscape. Over the years, how humanity seeks information has fundamentally transformed. The mechanics of discovery have grown infinitely more complex, and user behaviors have shifted toward new horizons.

As IAC continues to sharpen its overarching focus, we realized that maintaining a legacy search engine was no longer the best way to serve the future. It is a decision made not out of defeat, but out of a desire for clarity and forward momentum.

What This Means for You

Transparency and respect for your digital footprint are our top priorities during this transition. Please review the logistical steps we have taken effective May 1, 2026:

Service Discontinuation

The Ask.com search engine, mobile applications, and browser extensions have been permanently disabled and removed from all app stores.

Data Privacy & Deletion

In full compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations, all identifiable user search histories, accounts, and saved data have been securely and permanently wiped from our active servers.
